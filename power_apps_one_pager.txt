# Power Apps Screen: scrPdfToc

## Controls
- txtSourcePath: Text input
- txtOutputFolder: Text input
- btnCreateToc: Button
- btnOpenLink: Button

## Formulas
btnCreateToc.OnSelect:
Set(varResult, Flow_AddPdfToc_Cloud.Run({sourcePath: txtSourcePath.Text, outputFolder: txtOutputFolder.Text}));
If(varResult.status = "OK", Notify("TOC created", NotificationType.Success), Notify("Error: " & Coalesce(varResult.error, "Unknown error"), NotificationType.Error));

btnOpenLink.DisplayMode:
If(IsBlankOrError(varResult.shareLink), Disabled, Edit)

btnOpenLink.OnSelect:
Launch(varResult.shareLink)
